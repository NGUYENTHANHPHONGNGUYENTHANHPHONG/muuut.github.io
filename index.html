Ginger Jam & The Soul of Hue

People often say Hue is melancholic, but in truth, it is simply serene – a place holding deep and tender memories. Some memories don’t need words. Just a warm teapot and a plate of ginger jam, and everything gently returns: the crackling firewood, mother’s careful hands stirring the pot, and children's laughter echoing in the Lunar New Year air.

GIA HUE’s Ginger Jam isn’t a luxury treat, but a gift wrapped in love and nostalgia – made in a family kitchen, with real fire, real hands, and real patience – just like cooking for our own loved ones.

A Taste You’ll Never Forget

❣️ Ginger Jam with a special touch – every bite awakens memories of Tet, of mothers by the fire, a warm jam plate, and hot tea on a breezy veranda.

GIA HUE’s Ginger Jam is made from native Hue ginger – known for its rich aroma, gentle spiciness, and sweet aftertaste – much like Hue people: profound yet modest, warm yet quiet.

GIA HUE's Commitments

✅ Made from authentic Hue ginger from Tuan village – naturally fragrant, soft, and thin-skinned
✅ No preservatives – no bleaching – no artificial coloring
✅ Cooked over a traditional wood stove using a handed-down family recipe
✅ Every batch is handcrafted with care – just like we make for our own family
